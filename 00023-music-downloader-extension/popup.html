About Appu Music Downloader

Version: 2.0

Description:
This extension detects when a song is played on supported music sites, captures the high-quality stream URL, and allows you to download the track with the correct filename.

Features:

  • Auto-detect currently playing song
  • Capture CDN links for each song
  • Download with progress indicator
  • Copy filename to clipboard
  • Multiple site support (easily extendable)
